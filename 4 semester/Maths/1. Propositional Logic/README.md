# Some Important Law
- 1 Idempotent law
```
p  /\  p e p and p v p ⇒ p
```
- 2 Commutative law
 ```
p /\ q ⇒ q /\ p and p v q ⇒ q v p
```
- 3 Associative law
```
(p v q) v r = p v (q v r)
and (p A q) A r = p A (q A r)

```
- 4 De-Morgan law
```
~(p /\ q) ⇒ ~p V ~q
and ~(p v q) ⇒ ~p /\ ~q
```
- 5  p --> q ⇒ ~p /\ ~q
